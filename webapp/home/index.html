Services We Offer

Online 1:1 Live Sessions

Bridge of Music offers personalized online live classes via Google Meet, providing one-on-one guitar and piano lessons. These private sessions ensure quality music education with personal attention and doubt-solving assistance, progressing step-by-step.

Offline Group Classes

Bridge of Music also offers group sessions in their offline classrooms. These classes provide high-quality guitar and piano education in a collaborative environment, with personal attention and doubt-solving support, following a structured, step-by-step approach.
